Great Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon

Elevate your dinner with Grilled Lemon Herb Salmon, a dish that combines succulent salmon fillets with zesty citrus flavors and aromatic herbs for a delightful culinary experience.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 25 minutes.
  • Preparation: 15 minutes.
  • Cooking: 10 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, and thyme), salt, and black pepper.
  2. Marinate the salmon: Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish or resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the salmon, ensuring each fillet is evenly coated. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 15 minutes, or up to 1 hour, turning occasionally.
  3. Preheat the grill: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 400°F or 200°C). Brush the grates with oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Grill the salmon: Remove the salmon from the marinade and discard any excess marinade. Place the salmon fillets on the preheated grill, skin-side down. Close the lid and grill for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Serve: Transfer the grilled salmon to a serving platter and garnish with lemon slices and additional chopped herbs, if desired.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, accompanied by your favorite side dishes, such as roasted vegetables or a fresh green salad.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets , skin-on or skinless.
  • 1/4 cup olive oil.
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice.
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh herbs (such as dill, parsley, and thyme).
  • 1 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper.
  • Lemon slices and additional chopped herbs, for garnish.
